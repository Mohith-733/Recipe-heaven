Chingri Malai Curry

Ingredients:

  • 500 grams prawns, cleaned
  • 1 cup coconut milk
  • 1 onion (finely chopped)
  • 1 tablespoon ginger-garlic paste
  • 2 green chilies
  • 1 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 2 tablespoons mustard oil
  • Salt, to taste

Instructions:

  1. Heat mustard oil in a pan. Add onions and saut√© until golden.
  2. Add ginger-garlic paste, green chilies, and turmeric. Cook for a minute.
  3. Add prawns and salt. Cook until they turn pink.
  4. Pour in coconut milk and simmer for 5-7 minutes.
  5. Serve hot with rice.
