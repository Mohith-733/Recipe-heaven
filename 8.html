Tandoori Chicken

Ingredients:

  • 500g chicken (with skin, cut into pieces)
  • 1 cup yogurt
  • 2 tablespoons lemon juice
  • 2 tablespoons ginger-garlic paste
  • 2 teaspoons tandoori masala
  • 1 teaspoon red chili powder
  • 1 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh cilantro for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Marinate chicken with yogurt, lemon juice, ginger-garlic paste, tandoori masala, red chili powder, turmeric, and salt for at least 4 hours.
  2. Preheat grill/oven to 200°C (400°F).
  3. Place marinated chicken on skewers or directly on the grill. Cook for 30-35 minutes, turning occasionally.
  4. Garnish with cilantro and serve with mint chutney and lemon wedges.
