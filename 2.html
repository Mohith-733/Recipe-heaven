Chole Bhature

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup chickpeas (soaked overnight)
  • 1 large onion (finely chopped)
  • 2 tomatoes (pureed)
  • 1 teaspoon ginger-garlic paste
  • 2 teaspoons chole masala
  • 1 teaspoon cumin seeds
  • 2 green chilies (slit)
  • Salt to taste
  • Oil for cooking
  • 2 cups all-purpose flour (for bhature)
  • 1/2 cup yogurt (for bhature)
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder (for bhature)
  • Water as needed (for bhature)
  • Oil for frying (for bhature)

Instructions:

  1. Cook soaked chickpeas until soft in a pressure cooker.
  2. In a pan, heat oil and add cumin seeds. Saut√© onions until golden.
  3. Add ginger-garlic paste, green chilies, and cook for a minute.
  4. Add tomato puree, chole masala, and cooked chickpeas. Simmer for 15 minutes.
  5. For bhature, mix flour, yogurt, baking powder, and salt. Knead and rest for 30 minutes.
  6. Roll bhature and deep fry until golden. Serve with chole.
