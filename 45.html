Malpua

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 cup coconut (grated)
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 1/2 teaspoon cardamom powder
  • Oil, for frying

Instructions:

  1. Mix flour, coconut, milk, sugar, and cardamom to form a smooth batter.
  2. Heat oil in a pan, pour batter to form small pancakes.
  3. Fry until golden brown on both sides.
  4. Serve warm with sugar syrup or honey.
