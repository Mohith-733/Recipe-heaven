Biryani

Ingredients:

  • 500g chicken/mutton (cut into pieces)
  • 2 cups basmati rice
  • 2 large onions (sliced)
  • 2 tomatoes (chopped)
  • 1 cup yogurt
  • 2 tablespoons ginger-garlic paste
  • 2 teaspoons biryani masala
  • 4-5 green chilies (slit)
  • Whole spices (2-3 green cardamoms, 2-3 cloves, 1 stick cinnamon)
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh mint and cilantro for garnish
  • Oil or ghee for cooking

Instructions:

  1. Marinate meat with yogurt, ginger-garlic paste, biryani masala, green chilies, and salt for at least 1 hour.
  2. Wash and soak basmati rice for 30 minutes. Boil in salted water until 70% cooked, then drain.
  3. In a large pot, heat oil or ghee. Fry sliced onions until golden, reserve half.
  4. Add marinated meat, cook until browned, then add chopped tomatoes and cook until soft.
  5. Layer half the rice over the meat, sprinkle mint, cilantro, and half the fried onions. Add remaining rice and top with onions.
  6. Cover tightly and cook on low heat for 20-25 minutes.
  7. Gently fluff and serve with raita.
