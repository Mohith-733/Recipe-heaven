Bihari Kheer

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup rice
  • 4 cups milk
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 1/4 cup khoya
  • Cardamom powder, for flavor

Instructions:

  1. Wash and soak rice for 30 minutes.
  2. Boil milk in a pan, add soaked rice, and cook until soft.
  3. Add sugar and khoya, and cook for a few more minutes.
  4. Sprinkle cardamom powder and serve warm or chilled.
