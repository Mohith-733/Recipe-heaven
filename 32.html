Pav Bhaji

Ingredients:

  • 4-5 boiled potatoes
  • 1 cup mixed vegetables (peas, carrots, bell peppers)
  • 1 onion (chopped)
  • 2 tomatoes (chopped)
  • 2 teaspoons pav bhaji masala
  • Butter, for cooking
  • Pav (bread rolls)
  • Coriander leaves for garnish

Instructions:

  1. In a pan, heat butter, add onions and saut√© until golden.
  2. Add tomatoes, mixed vegetables, and pav bhaji masala; cook until soft.
  3. Mash the mixture and add boiled potatoes; mix well.
  4. Serve hot with buttered pav and garnish with coriander.
