Sambar

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup toor dal
  • 1 cup mixed vegetables
  • 1-2 tablespoons sambar powder
  • 1 teaspoon mustard seeds
  • Salt, to taste

Instructions:

  1. Pressure cook toor dal until soft.
  2. Cook mixed vegetables with turmeric and salt.
  3. Add mashed dal, sambar powder, and simmer.
  4. Temper with mustard seeds and serve hot.
