Chole

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup chickpeas (soaked overnight)
  • 1 onion (chopped)
  • 2 tomatoes (pureed)
  • 2 teaspoons chole masala
  • 1 teaspoon ginger-garlic paste
  • Salt, to taste
  • Oil, for cooking

Instructions:

  1. Pressure cook soaked chickpeas until soft.
  2. In a pan, heat oil, add onions, and saut√© until golden.
  3. Add ginger-garlic paste, tomato puree, and chole masala; cook for a few minutes.
  4. Add cooked chickpeas and simmer for 10-15 minutes.
  5. Serve hot with bhature or rice.
