Idli

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup urad dal
  • 2 cups idli rice
  • 1 teaspoon fenugreek seeds (optional)
  • Salt, to taste
  • Water, as needed

Instructions:

  1. Soak urad dal and fenugreek seeds for 6 hours. Soak the rice separately.
  2. Grind the dal to a smooth paste, and the rice to a coarse texture.
  3. Mix both pastes, add salt, and let it ferment overnight.
  4. Steam in idli molds for 10-12 minutes.
  5. Serve hot with chutney and sambar.
