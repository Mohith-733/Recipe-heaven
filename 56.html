Pani Puri

Ingredients:

  • 25 puris (store-bought or homemade)
  • 1 cup boiled and mashed potatoes
  • 1 cup chickpeas (boiled)
  • 1/2 cup tamarind chutney
  • 1/2 cup pani puri water (spiced water)
  • Coriander leaves for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Mix boiled potatoes, chickpeas, and tamarind chutney in a bowl.
  2. Gently break the top of each puri and fill with the potato mixture.
  3. Serve with pani puri water and garnish with coriander leaves.
