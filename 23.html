Dalma

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup split yellow dal (moong dal)
  • 1 cup mixed vegetables (pumpkin, potatoes, carrots)
  • 1 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1 teaspoon cumin seeds
  • 2 tablespoons ghee
  • Salt, to taste
  • 1 tablespoon ginger (grated)

Instructions:

  1. Cook dal with turmeric and salt until soft.
  2. In a separate pan, heat ghee. Add cumin seeds and ginger.
  3. Add mixed vegetables and saut√© for a few minutes.
  4. Add cooked dal and mix well. Simmer for 5-10 minutes.
  5. Serve hot with rice.
