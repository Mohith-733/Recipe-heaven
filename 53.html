Bhujia

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup besan (gram flour)
  • 1/4 cup potato (grated)
  • 1 teaspoon ajwain (carom seeds)
  • 1/2 teaspoon red chili powder
  • Salt, to taste
  • Oil, for frying

Instructions:

  1. In a bowl, mix besan, grated potato, ajwain, chili powder, and salt.
  2. Add water gradually to form a dough.
  3. Use a bhujia maker or sieve to shape the dough into thin strands over hot oil.
  4. Fry until golden and crispy. Drain on paper towels and serve as a snack.
