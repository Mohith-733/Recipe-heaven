Momos

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup minced vegetables (cabbage, carrots, etc.)
  • 1 tablespoon soy sauce
  • 1 teaspoon ginger-garlic paste
  • Salt, to taste

Instructions:

  1. Prepare dough with flour and water. Let it rest.
  2. Mix minced vegetables with soy sauce, ginger-garlic paste, and salt.
  3. Roll out dough, fill with the vegetable mix, and shape into momos.
  4. Steam for 10-15 minutes and serve with dipping sauce.
