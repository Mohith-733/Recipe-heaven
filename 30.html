Patra

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup colocasia leaves
  • 1/2 cup gram flour (besan)
  • 1 tablespoon jaggery (grated)
  • 1 teaspoon ginger paste
  • Salt, to taste

Instructions:

  1. Mix gram flour, jaggery, ginger, and salt to form a paste.
  2. Spread the paste on colocasia leaves and roll them tightly.
  3. Steam the rolls for 20-25 minutes and slice them.
  4. Serve with chutney.
