Rajasthani Dal Baati Churma

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup split yellow lentils (dal)
  • 2 cups whole wheat flour (for baati)
  • 1/2 cup jaggery (for churma)
  • 1 teaspoon ghee
  • Salt, to taste

Instructions:

  1. Prepare dal by boiling with salt and spices.
  2. Make a dough with whole wheat flour and form round balls (baati).
  3. Bake baati until golden brown.
  4. Crush baati with jaggery to make churma.
  5. Serve dal with baati and churma.
