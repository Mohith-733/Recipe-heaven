Bhutte Ka Kees

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups grated corn (fresh or frozen)
  • 1 tablespoon ghee
  • 1 onion (finely chopped)
  • 2 green chilies (finely chopped)
  • 1/2 teaspoon cumin seeds
  • Salt, to taste
  • Fresh coriander for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Heat ghee in a pan, add cumin seeds, and let them splutter.
  2. Add chopped onions and green chilies; saut√© until onions are translucent.
  3. Add grated corn and salt. Cook for 10-15 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  4. Garnish with fresh coriander and serve hot.
