Pitha

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups rice flour
  • 1 cup grated coconut
  • 1/2 cup jaggery (grated)
  • 1/2 teaspoon cardamom powder
  • Water, as needed
  • Banana leaves or steaming tray

Instructions:

  1. Mix rice flour, grated coconut, jaggery, cardamom powder, and enough water to form a dough.
  2. Shape the dough into small dumplings and wrap in banana leaves.
  3. Steam for about 20-25 minutes until cooked through.
  4. Serve warm as a snack or dessert.
