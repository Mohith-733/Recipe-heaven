Vada Pav

Ingredients:

  • 4-5 boiled potatoes (mashed)
  • 1 teaspoon mustard seeds
  • 1 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1 cup besan (gram flour)
  • 1/2 teaspoon red chili powder
  • Pav (bread rolls)
  • Chutney (green or tamarind)

Instructions:

  1. Heat oil in a pan, add mustard seeds, and let them splutter.
  2. Add mashed potatoes, turmeric, and red chili powder; mix well.
  3. Shape into balls, dip in besan batter, and deep fry until golden.
  4. Serve hot in pav with chutney.
