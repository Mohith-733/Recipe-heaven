Pakhala Bhata

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups cooked rice
  • 4 cups water
  • Salt, to taste
  • 1 onion (sliced)
  • 1 green chili (slit)
  • Coriander leaves for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Mix cooked rice with water and let it soak for a few hours.
  2. Add salt, sliced onion, and green chili to the soaked rice.
  3. Garnish with coriander leaves and serve cold.
