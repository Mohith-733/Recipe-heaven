Paneer Tikka

Ingredients:

  • 250g paneer (cubed)
  • 1 cup yogurt
  • 1 tablespoon ginger-garlic paste
  • 2 tablespoons lemon juice
  • 2 teaspoons garam masala
  • 1 teaspoon red chili powder
  • 1 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • Salt to taste
  • Bell peppers and onions (optional)
  • Skewers (wooden or metal)

Instructions:

  1. Marinate paneer with yogurt, ginger-garlic paste, lemon juice, garam masala, red chili powder, turmeric, and salt for at least 1 hour.
  2. Preheat grill/oven to 200°C (400°F).
  3. Thread marinated paneer and vegetables onto skewers.
  4. Grill for 15-20 minutes, turning occasionally, until charred.
  5. Serve hot with mint chutney and lemon wedges.
