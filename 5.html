Rogan Josh

Ingredients:

  • 500g lamb or goat meat (bone-in pieces)
  • 2 large onions (sliced)
  • 2 tablespoons ginger-garlic paste
  • 2 teaspoons red chili powder
  • 1 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1 teaspoon garam masala
  • 4-5 green cardamom pods
  • 4-5 cloves
  • 1 stick cinnamon
  • 1 cup yogurt
  • Salt to taste
  • Oil for cooking
  • Fresh cilantro for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Marinate meat with yogurt, ginger-garlic paste, chili powder, turmeric, and salt for at least 1 hour.
  2. In a pot, heat oil. Fry sliced onions until golden.
  3. Add marinated meat and cook until browned. Stir in cardamom, cloves, and cinnamon.
  4. Add water (1-2 cups) and simmer until meat is tender (1-2 hours).
  5. Add garam masala and simmer for another 10 minutes.
  6. Garnish with cilantro and serve with rice or naan.
