Dal Bafla

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup whole wheat flour
  • 1/4 cup ghee
  • Salt, to taste
  • 2 cups cooked dal (lentils)
  • Garam masala, for flavor

Instructions:

  1. Mix flour, ghee, and salt to form a dough.
  2. Shape into round balls and boil in water until cooked.
  3. Roast the boiled balls in ghee until golden brown.
  4. Serve with dal and a sprinkle of garam masala.
