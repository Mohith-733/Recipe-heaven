Bhel Puri

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups puffed rice
  • 1 onion (chopped)
  • 1 tomato (chopped)
  • 1 cucumber (chopped)
  • 1/4 cup sev (crispy noodles)
  • Chutneys (tamarind and green)
  • Coriander leaves for garnish

Instructions:

  1. In a bowl, mix puffed rice, onion, tomato, cucumber, and chutneys.
  2. Top with sev and garnish with coriander.
  3. Serve immediately as a snack.
