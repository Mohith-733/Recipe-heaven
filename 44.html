Chhat (Indore Style)

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup rice flour
  • 1 cup water
  • Salt, to taste
  • Oil, for frying
  • Chutneys (tamarind and green) for serving

Instructions:

  1. Mix rice flour, water, and salt to form a batter.
  2. Heat oil in a pan; pour batter to make small pancakes.
  3. Fry until golden on both sides.
  4. Serve hot with chutneys.
