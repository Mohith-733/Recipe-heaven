Puran Poli

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup chana dal (split chickpeas)
  • 1 cup whole wheat flour
  • 1/2 cup jaggery (grated)
  • 1/2 teaspoon cardamom powder
  • Ghee, for cooking

Instructions:

  1. Boil chana dal until soft and mash it.
  2. Mix with jaggery and cardamom to form a filling.
  3. Make a dough with flour, roll out, fill with the mixture, and seal.
  4. Cook on a griddle with ghee until golden.
