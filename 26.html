Litti Chokha

Ingredients:

  • For Litti:
  • 2 cups whole wheat flour
  • 1/2 cup sattu (roasted gram flour)
  • 1 tablespoon mustard oil
  • Salt, to taste
  • For Chokha:
  • 2 eggplants
  • 2 tomatoes
  • 1 onion (chopped)
  • 2 green chilies (chopped)
  • 1 tablespoon mustard oil

Instructions:

  1. Mix flour, salt, and water to make a dough for Litti.
  2. Prepare filling with sattu, chopped onions, and mustard oil.
  3. Shape Litti and bake until golden.
  4. Roast eggplants and tomatoes for Chokha, then mash and mix with other ingredients.
  5. Serve Litti with Chokha.
