Khar

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups raw papaya (cubed)
  • 1 cup water
  • 1 teaspoon alkaline water (lye water)
  • 2 green chilies (slit)
  • Salt, to taste

Instructions:

  1. Boil papaya in water until soft.
  2. Add alkaline water and green chilies, and simmer for a few minutes.
  3. Add salt to taste and serve hot with rice.
