Kachori

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 cup split yellow lentils (soaked and ground)
  • 1 teaspoon cumin seeds
  • 1/2 teaspoon red chili powder
  • Salt, to taste
  • Oil, for frying

Instructions:

  1. Mix flour with water and knead to form a dough.
  2. In a bowl, mix ground lentils, cumin, red chili powder, and salt.
  3. Divide the dough into balls, fill with the lentil mixture, and seal.
  4. Deep fry until golden brown. Serve hot with chutney.
