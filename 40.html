Gajar Halwa

Ingredients:

  • 4 cups grated carrots
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 1/4 cup khoya (reduced milk)
  • Cardamom powder, for flavor

Instructions:

  1. Cook grated carrots with milk in a pan until soft.
  2. Add sugar and khoya, and cook until thickened.
  3. Sprinkle cardamom powder and serve warm.
