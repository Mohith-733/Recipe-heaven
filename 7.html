Palak Paneer

Ingredients:

  • 250g paneer (cubed)
  • 4 cups spinach (washed and chopped)
  • 1 onion (chopped)
  • 2 tomatoes (pureed)
  • 1 tablespoon ginger-garlic paste
  • 2 green chilies (slit)
  • 1 teaspoon cumin seeds
  • 1 teaspoon garam masala
  • 1/2 cup cream
  • Salt to taste
  • Oil for cooking

Instructions:

  1. Blanch spinach in boiling water for 2-3 minutes, then drain and blend into a smooth paste.
  2. In a pan, lightly fry paneer cubes until golden. Remove and set aside.
  3. In the same pan, heat oil and add cumin seeds. Add chopped onions and saut√© until golden.
  4. Add ginger-garlic paste and green chilies. Stir in tomato puree and cook until oil separates.
  5. Add spinach puree and salt. Cook for 5-7 minutes.
  6. Stir in cream and garam masala. Add fried paneer cubes and simmer for another 5 minutes.
  7. Serve hot with roti or naan.
