Goan Fish Curry

Ingredients:

  • 500 grams fish (any white fish)
  • 1 onion (chopped)
  • 2 tomatoes (pureed)
  • 1 cup coconut milk
  • 2 teaspoons Goan curry powder
  • Salt, to taste
  • Oil, for cooking

Instructions:

  1. Heat oil in a pan, add onions, and saut√© until golden.
  2. Add pureed tomatoes, Goan curry powder, and salt; cook for a few minutes.
  3. Add fish and coconut milk; simmer until fish is cooked.
  4. Serve hot with rice.
