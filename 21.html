Macher Jhol

Ingredients:

  • 500 grams fish (any white fish)
  • 2 medium potatoes (cubed)
  • 1 onion (sliced)
  • 2 tomatoes (chopped)
  • 1 tablespoon ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1 teaspoon cumin seeds
  • Salt, to taste
  • 2 tablespoons mustard oil
  • Coriander leaves for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Heat mustard oil in a pan. Add cumin seeds and let them splutter.
  2. Add sliced onions and sauté until golden brown.
  3. Add ginger-garlic paste, turmeric, and sauté for a minute.
  4. Add potatoes and tomatoes, and cook until soft.
  5. Add fish, salt, and water. Simmer until the fish is cooked.
  6. Garnish with coriander leaves and serve hot with rice.
