Sooji Halwa

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup semolina (sooji)
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1/2 cup ghee
  • 2 cups water
  • 1/4 cup nuts (cashews, almonds)
  • 1/4 teaspoon cardamom powder

Instructions:

  1. In a pan, heat ghee and add semolina. Roast until golden brown.
  2. Add water and sugar; stir well until the mixture thickens.
  3. Add cardamom powder and nuts; mix well.
  4. Serve warm as a dessert.
