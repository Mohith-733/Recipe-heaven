Dhokla

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup besan (gram flour)
  • 1/2 cup yogurt
  • 1 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1 teaspoon ginger paste
  • 1 teaspoon green chili paste
  • 1 teaspoon Eno fruit salt
  • Salt, to taste
  • Coriander leaves for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Mix besan, yogurt, turmeric, ginger, chili paste, and salt to form a batter.
  2. Add Eno fruit salt just before steaming.
  3. Pour the batter into a greased steaming dish and steam for 15-20 minutes.
  4. Let it cool, cut into pieces, and garnish with coriander.
