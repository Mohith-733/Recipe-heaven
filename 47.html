Aloo Bhindi

Ingredients:

  • 2 medium potatoes (cubed)
  • 250 grams okra (bhindi, sliced)
  • 1 onion (sliced)
  • 1 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1 teaspoon cumin seeds
  • Salt, to taste
  • Oil, for cooking

Instructions:

  1. Heat oil in a pan, add cumin seeds, and let them splutter.
  2. Add sliced onions and saut√© until golden brown.
  3. Add potatoes, okra, turmeric, and salt; cook until vegetables are tender.
  4. Serve hot with roti or rice.
