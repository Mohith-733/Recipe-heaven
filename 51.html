Khar

Ingredients:

  • 250 grams raw papaya (grated)
  • 2-3 pieces of alkaline water (khar)
  • 1 onion (finely chopped)
  • 2-3 green chilies (slit)
  • Salt, to taste
  • Oil, for cooking
  • Coriander leaves for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Heat oil in a pan and saut√© onions until golden brown.
  2. Add grated papaya and green chilies; cook for a few minutes.
  3. Add the alkaline water (khar) and salt; mix well.
  4. Cook until the papaya is tender. Garnish with coriander leaves.
