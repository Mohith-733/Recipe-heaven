Sandesh

Ingredients:

  • 1 liter full cream milk
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 1/2 teaspoon cardamom powder
  • Pistachios for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Boil milk and curdle it with lemon juice. Drain to get chenna.
  2. Knead chenna with sugar and cardamom until smooth.
  3. Shape into small discs and garnish with pistachios.
  4. Refrigerate before serving.
