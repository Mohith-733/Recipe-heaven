Kebabs

Ingredients:

  • 500 grams minced meat (chicken or lamb)
  • 1 onion (finely chopped)
  • 2-3 green chilies (finely chopped)
  • 2 tablespoons ginger-garlic paste
  • 1/4 cup coriander leaves (chopped)
  • 1 teaspoon garam masala
  • Salt, to taste
  • Oil, for grilling

Instructions:

  1. In a bowl, mix minced meat, onion, green chilies, ginger-garlic paste, coriander, garam masala, and salt.
  2. Shape the mixture into kebabs.
  3. Heat oil on a grill or pan, and cook kebabs until golden brown on both sides.
  4. Serve hot with mint chutney.
