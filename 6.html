Aloo Gobi

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups cauliflower florets
  • 2 medium potatoes (cubed)
  • 1 onion (chopped)
  • 2 tomatoes (chopped)
  • 1 tablespoon ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 teaspoon cumin seeds
  • 1 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1 teaspoon red chili powder
  • 1 teaspoon garam masala
  • Salt to taste
  • Oil for cooking
  • Fresh cilantro for garnish

Instructions:

  1. In a pan, heat oil and add cumin seeds. Once they splutter, add chopped onions and sauté until translucent.
  2. Add ginger-garlic paste and sauté for a minute. Add tomatoes and cook until soft.
  3. Stir in turmeric, red chili powder, and salt. Add cubed potatoes and cauliflower florets. Mix well.
  4. Add a little water, cover, and cook on low heat until the vegetables are tender (about 15-20 minutes).
  5. Stir in garam masala and cook for another 5 minutes.
  6. Garnish with fresh cilantro and serve with roti or rice.
