Poha

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups flattened rice (poha)
  • 1 onion (chopped)
  • 2 green chilies (slit)
  • 1/2 teaspoon mustard seeds
  • 1/4 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • Coriander leaves for garnish
  • Salt, to taste
  • Oil, for cooking

Instructions:

  1. Wash the poha in water and drain it. Set aside.
  2. Heat oil in a pan, add mustard seeds, and let them splutter.
  3. Add onions and green chilies; saut√© until onions are translucent.
  4. Add turmeric powder and the drained poha. Mix well and cook for 5 minutes.
  5. Garnish with coriander leaves and serve hot.
